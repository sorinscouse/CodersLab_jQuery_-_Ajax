Iron Man
Iron Man is a wealthy American business magnate, playboy, and ingenious scientist, who fights evil with a set of powered suits of armor of his own design, equipped in state-of-the-art technologies. He is a member of an elite group of superheroes called the Avengers.
Thor
Thor was created by editor-plotter Stan Lee, scripter Larry Lieber, and penciller-plotter Jack Kirby. He has starred in several ongoing series and limited series, and is a founding member of the superhero team the Avengers, appearing in each volume of that series. The character is based on Thor, a god from Norse mythology.                 Thor is one of the founders of the Avengers, a member of the group in all four editions of the comic.
Wolverine
Wolverine is a mutant who possesses animal-keen senses, enhanced physical capabilities, powerful regenerative ability known as a healing factor, and three retractable claws in each hand. Wolverine has been depicted variously as a member of the X-Men, Alpha Flight, and the Avengers.
